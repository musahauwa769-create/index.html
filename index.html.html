Copywriting System

Every caption has
a job to do.

PAS — Authority Post

P
Problem

Open with a specific, painful truth the reader already feels.

A
Agitate

Make the cost of that problem real — time, money, team morale.

S
Solution

Name what Workpedia does and exactly how it fixes the problem.

Example Hook

"That role has been open for 60 days. Your team is covering the gap. The right person applied three weeks ago. You never saw them."

AIDA — Conversion Post

A
Attention

One sentence that stops the scroll. A number, a fact, a counter-intuitive truth.

I
Interest

Build the picture. Three short lines of context that earn the next read.

D
Desire

Name the result they want. Not what we do — what they get.

A
Action

One clear CTA. Specific. Actionable. No vague "reach out."

Example CTA

"DM us the role title and location today. We will send you a recruitment plan within 24 hours."

Action Plan

First 30 days.

The first 30 days are about laying the foundation right — brand voice locked, content system running, and the first leads coming in before the month is out.

Week 1 — Brand Foundation

  • Audit all existing profiles and align to brand voice
  • Lock bio, link in bio, profile photo and cover across all platforms
  • Produce first 7 pieces of content in advance
  • Define content pillars and assign weekly posting rhythm

Week 2 — Content Launch

  • Begin daily posting on LinkedIn and Instagram
  • Launch first engagement post — question or poll
  • Start daily comment and DM engagement routine
  • Publish first service spotlight post with direct CTA

Week 3 — Optimise & Engage

  • Review top-performing content and double down on format
  • Begin TikTok posting — 3x per week
  • Launch Facebook community group and seed first conversations
  • Run first boosted post on Facebook with ₦5,000–₦10,000 budget

Week 4 — Scale & Convert

  • Review DM pipeline — how many conversations started?
  • Publish first client result / social proof post
  • Test Instagram Reel with recruitment story hook
  • Compile Month 1 report and set Month 2 targets
